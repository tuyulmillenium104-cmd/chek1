Most people chase the next moonshot token. The real game is building systems where your capital compounds through influence.

MarbMarket is about to launch the first veDEX on MegaETH. No presale. No VCs. Just pure community-driven governance.

Here's how it works: You lock MARB tokens, get veTokens with voting power. The longer you lock, the more influence you have. Each week, veHolders vote on which LP pools get MARB emissions.

Projects want those votes? They pay bribes directly to veToken holders. This creates a flywheel: more locks → more votes → more bribes → higher rewards for lockers.

It's ve(3,3) in action - your capital doesn't just earn yield, it earns governance power that attracts more yield.

This isn't just another DEX. It's a shift from "provide liquidity, get rewards" to "provide liquidity, vote on rewards, and capture protocol value."

Fair launch means early adopters get asymmetric upside as the ecosystem grows. No seed rounds, no private allocations - just you, your capital, and your votes.

The veDEX model rewrites the rules of yield farming. Your capital compounds through governance, not just fees.
