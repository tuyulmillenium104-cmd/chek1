Most DEXes fight for liquidity. MarbMarket flips the script—letting liquidity providers vote for rewards. First veDEX on MegaETH, no VCs, no presale. Pure community power.

Here's how it works: lock MARB tokens → gain voting power → weekly votes determine which LP pools get MARB emissions. Projects can "bribe" for votes with extra incentives. Your lock duration = your influence.

The beauty? Longer locks mean more rewards AND more say. ve(3,3) model in action. MARB lockers direct emissions → LPs get rewards → protocols pay bribes → incentives flow back to lockers. Positive feedback loop.

This isn't just another DEX. It's a governance experiment. Community decides winners. No central authority. Just code, votes, and rewards.
