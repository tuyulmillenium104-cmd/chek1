MarbMarket is dropping the first veDEX on MegaETH. No presale. No VCs. Just a fair launch inspired by Solidly/Aerodrome.

A veDEX isn't your average DEX. Here's how it works: you lock MARB tokens to get veTokens. The longer you lock, the more voting power you have. Every week, veMARB holders vote on which LP pools get rewarded with emissions.

Projects can pay "bribes" to attract votes. This creates a wild incentive loop where liquidity providers get rewards, protocols pay for votes, and MARB lockers collect the benefits. It's a self-sustaining system.
