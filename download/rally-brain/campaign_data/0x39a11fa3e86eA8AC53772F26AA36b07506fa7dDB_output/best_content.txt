Most DEXs ignore the core problem: liquidity doesn't stay where it's needed. veDEX flips this by letting token holders decide where rewards go.

MarbMarket is launching the first veDEX on MegaETH—a fair launch with no presale or VC backing. Here's how it works:

Lock MARB tokens → gain veTokens with voting power. Longer locks = more influence + more rewards.

Every week, veHolders vote on which LP pools get MARB emissions. Projects can offer bribes to attract votes—creating a decentralized incentive system.

The MARB flywheel works like this: Lockers direct emissions → LPs get rewards → Protocols pay bribes for votes → Incentives flow back to MARB Lockers.

It's a community-driven model that aligns incentives. No more centralized decisions about where liquidity goes.
