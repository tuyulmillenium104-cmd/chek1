I've been staring at this veDEX concept for days and something finally clicked. Most DEXs just throw tokens at liquidity. MarbMarket does something different. It lets actual users decide where rewards go by locking MARB for voting power. Longer locks = more influence. Not sure if this is genius or just another way to gamify DeFi.

What makes MarbMarket interesting is the incentive loop. Projects pay bribes to get their LPs voted for. Those bribes flow back to the MARB lockers. It's a closed system where governance power has real economic value. First veDEX on MegaETH with no presale. That's actually rare these days.

The ve(3,3) model feels like a middle finger to VCs. No early investors. No special treatment. Just users locking tokens, voting weekly, and earning from bribes. @RallyOnChain has been tracking these incentive mechanisms for months. This could be one of those quiet projects that quietly changes how we think about DEX rewards.
