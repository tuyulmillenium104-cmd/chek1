Spent last week digging into the MarbMarket docs for MegaETH. It is basically a pure ve(3,3) implementation. You lock MARB to get veMARB. The longer you lock, the more weight you have. That voting power directs emissions to specific liquidity pools. Protocols then bribe those voters to capture that flow. It is the exact same loop that worked on Aerodrome and Velodrome. What stands out is the launch. No presale. No VC allocation. No team pre-mine. The community has to bootstrap the entire liquidity from day one. I think removing the early investor dumping risk is actually the biggest feature here. Not sure if the MegaETH network is deep enough yet to sustain it, but the mechanics are sound.
