I spent the weekend digging into MarbMarket on MegaETH. It is the first veDEX there and the mechanics are actually pretty interesting. You lock MARB to get veMARB. Longer lock means more voting power. Then you vote on which liquidity pools get emissions. Projects bribe you for those votes. This is the classic ve(3,3) model we saw work on Aerodrome and Velodrome. What stands out is the fair launch. No presale. No VCs. No team allocation. The community gets all the tokens from the start. That removes the early investor dumping risk that kills so many protocols. I think the token utility is solid if the volume shows up. Locking creates scarcity. Voting directs incentives. Bribes add yield. It all compounds. @RallyOnChain caught my attention with this one. Not sure if MegaETH has the liquidity yet to support it, but the setup is right.
