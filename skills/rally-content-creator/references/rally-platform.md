# Rally.fun Platform Knowledge Base

## What is Rally.fun?

Rally is the **first AI-powered, onchain marketing protocol** where anyone can earn by posting on X (Twitter). Built on:

- **GenLayer** — AI-powered blockchain for intelligent contract execution
- **LayerZero** — Omnichain interoperability protocol
- **Base** — Coinbase's L2 blockchain
- **ZKsync** — Zero-knowledge rollup scaling solution

## How Rally.fun Works

### For Creators
1. Connect your X/Twitter account to https://app.rally.fun
2. Browse active campaigns from Web3 projects
3. Create content (tweets) about the campaign
4. Post on X/Twitter
5. Rally's AI judges score your content
6. Earn tokens/rewards based on your score

### For Projects
1. Create a marketing campaign on Rally
2. Define the campaign brief, scoring criteria, and reward pool
3. Creators participate by posting content
4. AI ensures quality scoring (anti-spam, anti-bot)
5. Only high-quality, authentic content gets rewarded

## AI Scoring System

Rally.fun uses AI to score submitted content across multiple dimensions:

- **Authenticity detection** — Identifies AI-generated vs human content
- **Relevance matching** — How well content matches the campaign brief
- **Quality assessment** — Grammar, clarity, engagement potential
- **Spam detection** — Filters out bot content, duplicate posts
- **Compliance checking** — Required mentions, no forbidden patterns

### Key Insight: The AI Judge is Smart
The AI specifically looks for patterns that indicate LLM-generated content:
- Overuse of certain vocabulary (the "banned words")
- Template-like sentence structures
- Generic, non-specific claims
- Corporate/formal tone
- Excessive positivity without substance

**Real human content wins.** The system is designed to reward authentic voices, not AI-generated marketing copy.

## Campaign Types

Campaigns on Rally.fun typically fall into these categories:

1. **Product Launch** — New protocol, feature, or partnership announcement
2. **Awareness** — General brand awareness and education
3. **Community Building** — Growing the project's social community
4. **Event Promotion** — Upcoming events, AMAs, conferences
5. **Partnership Announcement** — Cross-project collaboration announcements

## Content Strategy Tips

### What Scores Well
- Personal stories and experiences
- Specific, detailed observations
- Genuine questions and curiosity
- Humor and personality
- Timely reactions to news/events
- Unique perspectives on common topics

### What Scores Poorly
- Generic "X is the future" claims
- Copy-paste project descriptions
- Excessive hashtags and mentions
- Thread-style formatted content
- Corporate marketing language
- Content that could apply to any project
